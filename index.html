About Me

My name is Shenglin Chen, and I specialize in painting, 3D animation, and the fusion of technology and art as a medium of expression. By combining traditional painting techniques and creative concepts with emerging technologies, I strive to create innovative and meaningful works that resonate with a modern audience. My goal is to spark new ideas and emotional connections by exploring the intersection of classical artistry and cutting-edge advancements. In a world where technology evolves at a breakneck pace, I aim to ensure that art remains an integral part of this progression. Through my work, I encourage others to envision a harmonious relationship between art and technology, fostering a deeper appreciation for how these fields can complement and inspire one another in our ever-changing world.
